How it works

From a wall of notes to a voice session in under a minute.

1

Paste your notes

Open your chapter document. Select all, copy, paste. No file pickers, no friction.

2

Claude reads it

Claude Sonnet 4.6 generates a mind map, 15–25 quiz questions, and a custom tutor persona — all from your source material.

3

Study out loud

Tap the mic. Your tutor greets you and asks the first question. Talk naturally. The whole session feels like a conversation, not a form.

Why this exists

My sister-in-law is in medical school. She was using ChatGPT voice to quiz herself on chapter notes and hated it — the voice was robotic, and worse, it just parroted her answers back instead of actually evaluating them.

So we built her this. The voice quality comes from ElevenLabs' best-in-class TTS. The reasoning comes from Claude. The experience comes from not cutting corners.
